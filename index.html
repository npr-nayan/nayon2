Water Monitoring System

The Water Monitoring System is an innovative project developed to enhance water safety and resource management. This system uses IoT technology and advanced sensors to continuously monitor critical parameters such as water quality, temperature, pH levels, and turbidity. The data is collected in real-time and transmitted to a central server for analysis and reporting. Key actions performed by the system include real-time water quality assessment, automatic alerts when water quality falls below set safety thresholds, and comprehensive reporting on water usage patterns. The systemâ€™s goal is to ensure the availability of safe and clean water while promoting sustainable water management practices. By detecting contamination or anomalies early, the system helps reduce the risks of waterborne diseases and ensures timely intervention to maintain water safety. Additionally, this project is instrumental in raising public awareness about water conservation and optimizing resource use for local communities and municipalities.


Technologies Used:


IoT sensors, ESP32, Python, C++, cloud-based data storage, real-time monitoring software, and custom algorithms for water quality analysis.


Impact:


This project contributes significantly to public health by ensuring cleaner water supplies and enhances water conservation efforts through intelligent monitoring and timely alerts. It empowers local communities and organizations to make informed decisions on water safety and usage.
